About Milli's

Stores like Milli’s aim to combine an exceptional shopping experience with the ability to interact with and adopt cats from local shelters. The concept was adapted from that of a cat cafe, which originated in Japan but are growing in popularity in the United States.

Milli’s features handmade goods from local businesses and partners with the Wisconsin Humane Society for its adoptable cats. There will normally be between 4-12 cats at Milli’s at one time.

To prevent crowds that may cause stress to the cats, we limit the number of guests who can be in the store at one time. Because of this, reservations are strongly encouraged.

To help maximize the benefits we can provide to our cats, donations can be made either directly to Milli’s or to the Wisconsin Humane Society. We also love to work with volunteers; please contact us for details!
